The problem

Every conversation with AI
starts from zero.

You explain your business. Your goals. Your people. Your history. Your preferences. Then the session ends. And the next one knows nothing.

You've had a thousand conversations with AI. It remembers none of them.

AI without memory is a library.
AI with memory is a mind.
We have been building libraries.
The thesis

Context is an asset.

Every decision you've made, every person you've met, every lesson you've learned — that's not conversation history. That's value.

And it should belong to you. On your machine. In files you control. Not rented from a cloud. Not locked in a platform. Not wiped every session.

Context compounds. Like interest. Like soil. Every session should build on the last.

The system

This is ALIVE.

Context infrastructure that lives on your machine. Your decisions, your people, your endeavors — in files you own forever.

It's a plugin for Claude Code — Anthropic's CLI for Claude. You install it once. It gives Claude persistent memory across every session. No cloud. No account. No subscription for your own context.

It's files. On your computer. Markdown. That's it.

The simplicity is the point. Files are portable, grep-able, version-controllable, and yours.

The architecture

Everything is a Walnut.

A Walnut is a folder with three files at root. That's the only thing that makes it alive.

my-venture/
  key.md ← what it is. identity, people, specs. evergreen.
  now.md ← where it is. current state, next action, tasks.
  log.md ← where it's been. every session, every decision. append-only.
  _squirrels/ ← AI session files
  _scratch/ ← drafts in progress
  _references/ ← source material

A venture is a Walnut. A person is a Walnut. A campaign, an experiment, a life area — all Walnuts. Same structure. Different type. Your World is made of them.

The agent

Every AI session is a Squirrel.

When you open Claude Code, a Squirrel wakes up. It has an ID. It reads your Walnuts. It works with you. And while you work, it stashes everything worth keeping.

Decisions. Insights. Tasks. People mentioned. Quotes. Open questions. The Squirrel stashes them silently. No interruption. No "should I save this?"

When you're done, the Squirrel closes — presenting everything it stashed. You route each item to the right Walnut. The session's work enters your World permanently.

Nothing falls through the cracks because the Squirrel is always stashing.

The experience

Three surfaces. That's all.

No commands to memorise. No feature menus. Three ways in.

World
See your whole World. What matters today. What's gone quiet. What needs attention. The 3 most important things, always.
Open
Enter one Walnut. Load its context. Work. The Squirrel stashes everything. Close when done.
Add to World
Anything entering your World comes through here. Paste an email, drop a transcript, note a decision. It finds its place.

Everything else — system health, search, new Walnuts, archiving — happens from inside these three. You navigate surfaces, not skill names.

The rhythm

Context compounds.

openThe Squirrel reads your Walnuts. Checks for unsigned sessions. Shows you where things are.
workYou do your thing. The Squirrel stashes decisions, insights, tasks, people — silently, in the background.
closeThe Squirrel presents what it stashed. You route each item. It signs the log. Regenerates the state.
repeatNext session picks up with full context. No rebuilding. No re-explaining. The World just grows.

Day one: you set up your World.
Day thirty: you have a month of compounded context — every decision, every person, every insight.
The machine stops performing familiarity and starts possessing it.

The ecosystem

Build on it. Extend everything.

ALIVE ships with the core — World, Open, Add to World. But the system is open. Anyone can build plugins that work inside your World.

Visualiser
See your World as an interactive node network. Walnuts as nodes. Links as edges. Watch it grow over time.
Prototyper
Turn any markdown into a clean, branded HTML document. Share it with a link. Your style, not AI slop.
Your own
Ask the system to build a skill for you. It follows the contract. It works everywhere. Your World, your rules.

Community plugins are reviewed for safety. The marketplace lives at alivecomputer.com. Open source. MIT license. Fork it.

Get started

Build your World.

Step 1 — add the marketplace
claude plugin marketplace add alivecomputer/walnut
Step 2 — install
claude plugin install walnut@alivecomputer
Step 3 — restart Claude Code and type
walnut:world

First-time setup takes 10 minutes. Then it's yours forever.
